# AI Video Generation Prompts

These prompts were designed to generate UGC-style marketing videos for a local salon using AI video tools like Runway, CapCut, and Pika Labs.

---

## Prompt 1 – Keratin Transformation Video

Create a realistic UGC-style vertical video showing a young woman entering a salon with frizzy hair. The stylist analyzes her hair and performs a keratin treatment. The final scene reveals smooth, shiny hair and the customer smiling happily.

Style: natural lighting, vlog-style camera  
Format: vertical 9:16 video  
Duration: 6–10 seconds

---

## Prompt 2 – Haircut Glow-Up

Create a vertical UGC-style video of a customer getting a layered haircut in a salon. The video should include before and after shots showing the transformation.

Style: casual, authentic, social media friendly.

---

## Prompt 3 – Budget Glow-Up

Create a quick UGC-style video showing a salon glow-up experience including facial treatment, hair styling, and final transformation.

Tone: friendly and relatable.

---

## Prompt 4 – Before and After Transformation

Create a split-screen transformation video showing frizzy hair before the treatment and smooth hair after the treatment.

Style: minimal, aesthetic, and modern.

---

## Prompt 5 – Customer Reaction

Create a selfie-style vlog video where a customer talks about their experience at the salon and shows the final haircut result.

Tone: authentic and conversational.
